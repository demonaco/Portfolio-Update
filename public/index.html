Get to know me


Hey there! I'm Matt, a full stack web developer who works with multiple technologies such as Javascript, HTML, CSS, Mongo DB, React and more on a daily basis. I live in beautiful San Francisco and absolutely love traveling!

My current background is in business development. I've lead production and management of coding projects from idea to full-complete working applications.

I received my Bachelors of Science in Music Industry with a minor in Cinematic Arts from the University of Southern California, and more recently have graduated with certification in Full Stack Web Development from the University of California, Berkeley.

Throughout this time I have learned the language of development by working closely with engineers and developers to find solutions to bugs, test for quality assurance purposes, and manage projects for developers to complete.

My passion is in music and technology, and my career focus is on building applications in either the audio or entertainment industry.

What I'll be learning next: Java, Python, and working towards fluency on my French.

Front-end technologies:

HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, React UI, Handlebars.

Back-end technologies:

Express, React backend, Node JS, Mongoose database, RESTful APIs, MySQL database, Sequelize, Google Firebase.
